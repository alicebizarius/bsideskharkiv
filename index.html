About BSides

BSides is a word framework for cyber security conferences. There are more than 400 conferences in all the world. BSides was born out of number of rejections to the CFP for Black Hat USA 2009. It was co-founded by Mike Dahn, Jack Daniel, and Chris Nickerson.
It's time for continue this framework in Kharkiv.
BSides Kharkiv Conference is conference for young enthusiasts in information security.
BSides Kharkiv Crew invites all of specialists, who are on the start of their career, who aren't indifferent in information security, who want to build strong community, to connect to this conference.

Our goals are
* to conect specialists and young enthusiasts for discussion of topical issues in the field of information security
* to establish interaction and cooperation between the participants of the conference
* to begin discussions between participants for for sharing experiences and ideas, raising awareness and building trust within the community

Our structure will have
* one stream for useful and actual information from experts in cyber security
* workshops. A unique opportunity to get practical recommendations and to supplement your knowledge on data protection and vulnerability search.

CALL FOR PAPERS

We are starting CFP! We want our community to grow and improve, so we are looking for new and actual reports.
Our conference is targeted at young and novice enthusiasts, so we do not expect high-tech and highly specialized reports. We want to present the most simple and useful content for newcomers who need to choose their own way and understand what knowledge they lack and what needs to be developed. Our conference, with your report, will help them to get a sense of information security and deciding whether or not they want to become part of the industry and community.
If you have an interesting, and at the same time simple and accessible topic that you want to share - take part in our CFP. All you need is just fill out the form!
